This work develops neural network building blocks for symmetric spaces of noncompact type, including a unified point-to-hyperplane distance formulation, fully connected layers, and attention mechanisms. The approach is evaluated on image classification, EEG signal classification, image generation, and natural language inference.

Recommended citation: Nguyen, X. S., Yang, S., & Histace, A. (2025). "Neural Networks on Symmetric Spaces of Noncompact Type." <i>International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR)</i>.
