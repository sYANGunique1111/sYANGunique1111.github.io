ADGT proposes a parallel GCN-transformer architecture for 2D-to-3D human pose lifting. It combines local skeleton modeling with global contextual reasoning through Hop-Wise Scalable Adaptive GCN, an Attention-Based Local Feature Extractor, and Register-Based Transformer Enhancement.
